How to Use

Welcome to the Random Task Generator! Here's how it works:

  • Click "Get a Random Task" to receive a task.
  • Complete the task to earn points. You can only complete the task after 1 minute.
  • You can get a new task every hour.
  • Customize the experience using the settings menu.
